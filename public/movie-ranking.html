The Best Movies According to Nicolson

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Alive (1993), Frank Marshall

This production tells the story of a plane crash that happened to the Uruguayan rugby team, Old Christians Club, in 1972.

A Brilliant Mind (2001), Ron Howard

Starring Russell Crowe, the film tells the true story of Nobel Prize-winning economist John Forbes Nash.

The Intouchables (2011), Olivier Nakache and Eric Toledano

In reality, the protagonist of the story, Philippe Pozzo di Borgo, was married to a wealthy heiress when he suffered the accident that left him in a wheelchair.
